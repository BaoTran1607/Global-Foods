COMPANY OVERVIEW
F-one Global Foods Co., Ltd. was established in 2015, is currently a Korean food processing factory, and also operates as an OEM/ODM factory. F-one Global Foods Company Limited is an FDI company with 100% investment capital from Korea. Our company belongs to the agricultural product processing and storage factory project with a total area of 19,312 m²: Factory No. 1 area of about 4,400m² and expected Factory No. 2 with an area of about 14,000m².
F-one Global Foods Co., Ltd. is located in Duc Chinh ward, Dong Trieu town, Quang Ninh province, located in the middle of the farming and agricultural production area. There is a large source of clean water available around and a favorable climate to develop experimental farming and agricultural product processing activities.

VISION AND MISSION
VISION

As the first Korean food manufacturing company in Northern Vietnam, we have the passion and desire to bring you and your family the freshest and highest quality products. With serious, long-term investment combined with modern technology and continuous improvement, we are determined to become a company with a famous food manufacturing brand: Trusted by brands and loved by consumers.

In the future, we will grow to become the best food manufacturing company in Vietnam.

MISSION

We always strictly require our partners and suppliers that input materials have clear origins, are safe and meet standards. Our production processes and products always comply with food safety and hygiene. Besides, we also continuously invest in modern machinery and train human resources to increasingly improve product quality.

Our products are exported to Korea, Japan, Canada, Australia, etc. We are going to export to the US market in 2024.
